About Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system and a gas giant. It is known for its Great Red Spot, a massive storm that has been raging for centuries.

Key Facts

Largest Planet In Our Solar System

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system, with a diameter of about 142,984 kilometers.

Great Red Spot

Jupiter's Great Red Spot is a massive, century-long storm, likely formed by a flow disturbance between opposing jet streams in the southern hemisphere.

Moons

Jupiter has at least 79 known moons, with the four largest being Io, Europa, Ganymede, and Callisto.
