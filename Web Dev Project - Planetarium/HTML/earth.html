Earth

Our home planet — the only known world to support life.

About Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. It has a diverse environment with water, land, and atmosphere that supports a wide range of ecosystems.

Key Facts

Abundant Liquid Water

Earth is often called the "Blue Planet" because it is the only place in the solar system known to have liquid water on its surface, covering about 71% of its area.

Unique Life-Supporting Atmosphere

Earth's atmosphere is composed of 78% nitrogen and 21% oxygen, providing the perfect balance to support life.

Active Geological Processes

Earth is not static, it has a dynamic interior that drives active plate tectonics, resulting in volcanoes and the continuous reshaping of its surface.
